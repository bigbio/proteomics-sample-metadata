invertebrates

v1.1.0 sample

SDRF template for invertebrate samples (Drosophila, C. elegans, insects, etc.). Must be combined with a technology template (ms-proteomics, affinity-proteomics, or ms-metabolomics).

Key Guidance

The invertebrates template covers Drosophila, C. elegans, insects, and other arthropods.

Key guidance:

  • characteristics[strain or breed]: Required. Use standard strain names

(Oregon-R, w1118, Canton-S for Drosophila; N2, Bristol for C. elegans).

  • characteristics[developmental stage]: Required. Use organism-specific ontologies:

FBdv for Drosophila, WBls for C. elegans, or general EFO/UBERON terms.

  • characteristics[genotype]: Optional. Use standard genetic notation

(wild type, daf-2(e1370), w[*]; P{GAL4}).

  • characteristics[disease]: Required.

Mutually exclusive with human, vertebrates, and plants.

Combination Rules
