water

v1.0.0 sample

SDRF template for aquatic metaproteomics. Extends metaproteomics with water-specific columns aligned with the GSC MIxS water extension (0016014). Combine with ms-proteomics for MS acquisition columns.

Key Guidance

The water template adds aquatic environment metadata from the MIxS water

extension. These columns describe water body properties, chemical/physical

measurements, and sampling context for aquatic microbiome studies.

PRIDE ontology terms (PRIDE:0000829 children) include MIxS cross-references.

Usage: water + ms-proteomics

Inheritance

base → metaproteomics → water

Combination Rules

  • Mutually exclusive with: human-gut, soil
